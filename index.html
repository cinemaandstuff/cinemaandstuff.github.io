My Passion For Cinema, Literature and Music

Everybody has some interests. It could be fashion, it could be art or it could be football.

My passion has started as cinema, however while cinema is becoming a thing; I've figured that there are other elements that create cinema in a specific way.

Cinema feeds on litearature. For example, A Clockwork Orange, Kubrick's famous movie was once a novel. Literature's steps on writing and cinema's steps on scripts are nearly same. A hero, then his journey basically.

Also without music, a diagetic world cannot be created. Remember Fight Club, at the ending scene, Where is My Mind plays and it changes its effect suddenly more are more.

Getting into cinema made me question all of these elements. I felt like I needed a knowledge in all of these stuff. Therefore, while understanding literature or music; their world were something else. Yes, there are some common parts but defining them only as cinema's elements would be a total contempt.
